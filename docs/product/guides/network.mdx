# Network

The network view shows you all the connected devices in your library, you can see their status, and even browse their filesystems.

Additionally, local network shares accessible from _any_ connected Spacedrive node will show here, allowing you to explore your extended local network.

## Contacts

Contacts are other Spacedrive users you have saved information for in your library. You can see their status and previously shared files from here.
